About Us



Welcome to Cossy Construction - Maintenance and Building Repairs, Your Trusted Partner for Quality Solutions in Kempton Park and Throughout
the Gauteng Region!






With over a decade of experience, Cossy Construction has established itself as a leading name in the construction and maintenance industry.
Headquartered in Kempton Park, we proudly serve the entire Kempton Park area and extend our expertise across the Gauteng region.




Our Commitment:

At Cossy Construction, our commitment to excellence is the cornerstone of our operations. We specialize in a comprehensive range of
services, including house construction, repairs, industrial maintenance, painting, plastering, and plumbing. Whether you're embarking on a
new project or need reliable maintenance and repair services, we are here to deliver top-notch solutions tailored to your needs.



Services We Offer:



House Construction: From concept to completion, we bring your dream home to life with precision and expertise.



Repairs: Our skilled team is equipped to handle repairs of all scales, ensuring that your property is well-maintained and structurally sound.



Industrial Repairs: Cossy Construction excels in industrial maintenance, providing reliable solutions to keep your industrial facilities
operating at peak efficiency.




Painting and Plastering: Enhance the aesthetics and durability of your property with our professional painting and plastering services.


Plumbing: Our plumbing services cover both industrial and domestic needs. From complex industrial plumbing projects to routine domestic
plumbing repairs, we've got you covered.





Service Area:

While our roots are in Kempton Park, Cossy Construction proudly extends its services throughout the entire Gauteng region. Our commitment
to quality knows no boundaries, and we are dedicated to delivering excellence to every client, regardless of their location.



Why Choose Cossy Construction:

Experience: With over 10 years in the industry, we bring a wealth of experience to every project.
Versatility: From construction to repairs, and from industrial maintenance to plumbing, we offer a diverse range of services to meet all
your needs.




Quality Assurance: At Cossy Construction, quality is non-negotiable. We prioritize excellence in every aspect of our work.
Partner with Cossy Construction for all your construction, maintenance, and repair needs. With us, you're not just getting a service;
